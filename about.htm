Stained Glass Technique

Tiffany Technique - A glass assembly technique invented by Louis Comfort Tiffany (1893), who was himself inspired by the ancient tradition of glassmaking.
The Tiffany technique stained glass is a method of forming a decorative design for home decorative items, windows, lamps, or even jewelry by wrapping the patterned pieces of glass with thin copper foil and then soldering them all together to create gorgeous art. It is a technique inspired by the marvelous cathedral windows.
